About Chia Pet

Welcome to the world of Chia Pet, where creativity grows!

Chia Pets are fun and unique decorative planters that allow you to grow plants in various shapes and sizes. Our journey began with the iconic Chia Pet Human and has since expanded to include a wide range of characters, animals, and celebrities.

"I wanted to make it foolproof and attractive, so that even a dummy could do it. And that's how I came up with Chia Pets." - Joseph Pedott

The intriguing "Chia Poll" phenomenon emerged, accurately predicting election outcomes based on Chia Pet sales, while Chia Pets themselves found a place in a time capsule, symbolizing their significance in 20th-century American culture. With a unique seasonal marketing strategy and enduring popularity, Chia Pets continue to charm and entertain, even inspiring innovative art forms like wearable Chia Pet-inspired garments.

Cultural Impact

Chia Pets have left an indelible mark on popular culture, becoming synonymous with nostalgia and whimsy. These quirky creations have been featured in movies, TV shows, and even art installations, solidifying their status as beloved icons of the 20th and 21st centuries.

Did You Know?

  • The famous "ch-ch-ch-Chia" jingle was born from a brainstorming session during a party.
  • Chia Pets have become a popular collectible and gift item.
  • The Chia Poll claimed to predict election outcomes.
  • Chia Pets are included in a time capsule at the American Museum of Natural History.
